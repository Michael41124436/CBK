About CBK

CBK Banking System is a secure banking prototype designed to help customers create accounts, log in using MPIN or biometric phrase, request loans, and transfer funds to partner banks in Kenya. Account creation and transfers require admin approval.

Our goal is to provide a streamlined, user-friendly interface for account management, profile access, and financial services.

  • Secure login options
  • Account creation with photo and identity uploads
  • Balance checking and transaction history
  • Loan request service and bank transfers
